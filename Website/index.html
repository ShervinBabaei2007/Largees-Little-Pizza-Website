How Do You Make Healthy Pizza?

Step 1: Make the Dough

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions:

  1. Mix water, yeast, and honey – let sit 5–10 minutes.
  2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil – mix into dough.
  3. Knead for 5–8 minutes.
  4. Let it rise for 1 hour in bowl.
  5. Roll it out into your preferred shape.

Step 2: Make the Sauce

  • 1 cup crushed tomatoes
  • 1 garlic clove (minced)
  • ½ tsp oregano
  • ½ tsp basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp olive oil (optional)

Simmer on low heat for 10–15 minutes.

Step 3: Add Toppings

  • Cheese: mozzarella, feta, or goat cheese
  • Veggies: spinach, peppers, mushrooms, etc.
  • Protein: chicken, tofu, turkey pepperoni
  • Extras: pineapple, arugula, chili flakes

Step 4: Assemble & Bake

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C)
  2. Spread sauce, cheese, and toppings
  3. Bake for 12–15 minutes until golden and bubbly

Step 5: Serve

  1. Let it cool for a few minutes
  2. Slice and serve with fresh toppings
  3. Enjoy your healthy pizza!
